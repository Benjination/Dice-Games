# Installing DiceGames on Your Phone

DiceGames is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that can be installed on your phone for a native app experience! No App Store needed.

## ✨ Benefits

- 📱 **Native app feel** - Runs full-screen without browser UI
- ⚡ **Fast loading** - Cached resources load instantly
- 🔌 **Works offline** - Play even without internet connection
- 🏠 **Home screen icon** - Launch directly from your phone
- 💾 **Less storage** - Smaller than a native app
- 🔄 **Always up-to-date** - Automatically updates when you visit

---

## 📱 iOS Installation (iPhone/iPad)

### Requirements
- **Safari browser** (this feature only works in Safari)
- iOS 11.3 or later

### Steps

1. **Open DiceGames in Safari**
   - Navigate to: https://benjination.github.io/Dice-Games/

2. **Tap the Share button**
   - Look for the share icon (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
   - On iPad, it's at the top of the screen

3. **Find "Add to Home Screen"**
   - Scroll down in the share menu
   - Tap "Add to Home Screen"

4. **Customize and Add**
   - You can edit the name if you want
   - Tap "Add" in the top right corner

5. **Done!**
   - The DiceGames icon will appear on your home screen
   - Tap it to launch the app

### Troubleshooting iOS
- **Don't see "Add to Home Screen"?** Make sure you're using Safari browser
- **Icon not appearing?** Try restarting your device
- **Page won't load?** Check your internet connection and try again

## 🤖 Android Installation

### Requirements
- Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet browser
- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later

### Method 1: Browser Prompt (Easiest)

1. **Open DiceGames in Chrome**
   - Navigate to: https://benjination.github.io/Dice-Games/

2. **Look for the install prompt**
   - A banner should appear saying "Add DiceGames to Home screen"
   - Tap "Install" or "Add"

3. **Done!**
   - The app will be added to your home screen

### Method 2: Manual Installation

1. **Open DiceGames in Chrome**
   - Navigate to: https://benjination.github.io/Dice-Games/

2. **Open browser menu**
   - Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner

3. **Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"**
   - The option may vary depending on your browser

4. **Confirm installation**
   - Tap "Add" or "Install"

5. **Done!**
   - The DiceGames icon will appear on your home screen

### Troubleshooting Android
- **Don't see install option?** Make sure you're using Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet
- **Banner didn't appear?** Use the manual method above
- **Installation failed?** Clear browser cache and try again

## 💻 Desktop Installation

### Chrome/Edge on Desktop

1. **Open DiceGames in Chrome or Edge**
   - Navigate to: https://benjination.github.io/Dice-Games/

2. **Look for the install icon**
   - You'll see a ⊕ or install icon in the address bar
   - Or click the three dots menu → "Install DiceGames"

3. **Click Install**
   - Confirm the installation

4. **Done!**
   - The app will open in its own window
   - Access it from your apps menu or taskbar

Once installed, DiceGames will:

- ✅ Appear in your app drawer/launcher
- ✅ Work offline for games you've already loaded

## 🔄 Updating the App

The app updates automatically! Just:
1. Open the app while connected to internet
2. It will check for and download updates in the background
3. Updates apply on next launch

You can also force an update by:
- Closing and reopening the app
- Or refreshing from Settings

## 🗑️ Uninstalling the App

### iOS
1. Long-press the DiceGames icon on your home screen
2. Tap "Remove App" → "Delete App"

### Android
1. Long-press the DiceGames icon
2. Drag to "Uninstall" or tap app info → "Uninstall"

### Desktop
1. Right-click the app icon
2. Select "Uninstall DiceGames"

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is this safe to install?**  
A: Yes! PWAs are installed through your browser's security sandbox. They can't access system files or other apps.

**Q: Will it use less storage than a native app?**  
A: Yes! PWAs typically use 1-5 MB, while native apps are often 50-200 MB.

**Q: Do I need to update it manually?**  
A: No! It updates automatically when you're connected to internet.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**  
A: Yes! Core features work offline. Some features (like saving to cloud) require internet.

**Q: Why install instead of just bookmarking?**  
A: Installed apps run full-screen, load faster, work offline, and feel like native apps.

**Q: Does it work on other browsers?**  
A: PWA installation works best in Safari (iOS), Chrome/Edge (Android/Desktop). Other browsers may have limited support.

**Q: Will my saved games sync between web and installed app?**  
A: Yes! Both use the same Firebase account, so your games sync automatically.
